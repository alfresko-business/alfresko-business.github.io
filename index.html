Luzon

Luzon is the largest and most populous island in the Philippines, known for its bustling cities, breathtaking natural landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

Visayas

Visayas is a group of islands in the central Philippines, known for its beautiful beaches, colorful festivals, and friendly people.

Mindanao

Mindanao is the second-largest island in the Philippines, located in the southern part of the country. It is known for its rich biodiversity, diverse culture, and stunning natural landscapes.

Random Fact of the Day

Banaue Rice Terraces was man-made

The construction of the Banaue Rice Terraces required a tremendous amount of physical labor and communal cooperation. It is said that the Ifugao people built the terraces through a system of mutual aid known as "bayanihan," where community members would help each other with the construction in exchange for reciprocal labor.
