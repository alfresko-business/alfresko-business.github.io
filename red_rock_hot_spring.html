Red Rock Hot Spring

Place: Valencia, Negros Oriental

What Makes It Unique: It is renowned for its geothermal hot springs, which are naturally heated by underground volcanic activity. The hot spring water flows into various pools, providing visitors with a soothing and therapeutic bathing experience.

Overview

Red Rock Hot Spring offers a unique and rejuvenating experience for visitors seeking relaxation and therapeutic benefits. In this educational content, we will delve into the captivating features of Red Rock Hot Spring and explore why it is a hidden gem worth exploring.

Red Rock Hot Spring is known for its geothermal hot springs, which are heated naturally by underground volcanic activity. The mineral-rich waters that flow into the pools offer numerous therapeutic benefits. The high mineral content, including calcium, magnesium, and sulfur, is believed to promote relaxation, ease muscle tension, improve circulation, and soothe various ailments. Visitors can immerse themselves in these healing waters, allowing the warmth and mineral infusion to revitalize their body and mind.

Red Rock Hot Spring boasts a serene and rustic natural setting, nestled amidst lush vegetation and perched on a cliffside. The tranquil ambiance allows visitors to connect with nature, providing a calming and peaceful environment for relaxation and contemplation. The surrounding trees and scenic vistas create an idyllic backdrop, enhancing the overall experience of rejuvenation and tranquility.

Unlike larger and more crowded tourist spots, Red Rock Hot Spring offers a more intimate and exclusive experience. With a smaller capacity, visitors can enjoy the hot springs in a private and secluded setting, providing a sense of privacy and relaxation. Whether seeking a romantic getaway or a peaceful retreat, the privacy and exclusivity of Red Rock Hot Spring ensure an undisturbed experience to unwind and recharge.

Red Rock Hot Spring presents a unique opportunity to immerse oneself in the healing waters of geothermal hot springs, surrounded by the tranquility of nature. Its rustic natural setting, and privacy and exclusivity, make it a hidden gem that offers an unforgettable and rejuvenating experience. Embrace the therapeutic benefits and natural beauty of Red Rock Hot Spring, and discover a sanctuary of relaxation and well-being.

Location

Red Rock Hot Spring is in the mountain town of Valencia, about 25 minutes drive from Dumaguete City. The best way to get around in Dumaguete is rent your own scooter for about ₱500 a day


If travelling in a larger group (more than 2) or aren’t confident riding a scooter, then a hire car is a great option. And with a hire car there is the option for a driver. Besides from driving you around, your driver will usually suggest a route and popular tourist attractions to include in the day. A hire car with driver costs ₱3000 for 8 hours and can take 4 passengers.


For public transport, catch a multi-cab heading to Valencia from near the public market. Let the driver know you are going to Red Rock Hot Springs so he will drop you off as close as possible. If you are too far away use a habal-habal for the rest of the journey.
