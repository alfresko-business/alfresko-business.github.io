Kawasan Falls

Place: Badian, Cebu

What Makes It Unique: The opportunity for canyoneering. Visitors can engage in an exhilarating adventure by jumping off cliffs, sliding down natural water slides, and swimming through canyons to reach the falls. This combination of adventure and natural beauty makes Kawasan Falls a unique destination for thrill-seekers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Overview

Kawasan Falls, located in Badian, Cebu, is a breathtaking natural attraction that captivates visitors with its turquoise cascades, lush surroundings, and adventurous opportunities. In this educational content, we will delve into the enchanting features of Kawasan Falls and explore why it is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Kawasan Falls is renowned for its captivating natural beauty. The falls consist of multiple tiers of cascading water, each displaying a vibrant turquoise hue that stems from the area's unique limestone rock formation. The water, flowing from the Mantalongon Mountain Range, creates a series of picturesque waterfalls, creating a stunning backdrop against the lush greenery and towering cliffs. The tranquil atmosphere and pristine surroundings make it a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

One of the most thrilling aspects of Kawasan Falls is the opportunity for canyoneering. Adventure seekers can embark on an adrenaline-pumping journey, jumping off cliffs, sliding down natural water slides, and swimming through canyons. The canyoneering experience takes you on a remarkable exploration of the area's natural wonders, allowing you to intimately connect with the power and beauty of nature. Proper safety precautions and experienced guides are available to ensure a safe and enjoyable adventure.

As you venture further upstream from the main waterfall, you will discover the serene Blue Lagoon and a series of natural pools. These tranquil havens offer a soothing respite, where visitors can relax, swim, and soak in the refreshing waters surrounded by lush vegetation. The azure blue shades of the lagoon create a captivating sight, making it a favorite spot for capturing Instagram-worthy moments and immersing oneself in the natural splendor of the surroundings.

Kawasan Falls plays a crucial role in supporting the local ecosystem. The Matutinao River system, of which the falls are a part, sustains a diverse range of plant and animal species. The abundant vegetation provides habitat for various bird species and other wildlife, making it an excellent destination for nature enthusiasts. Exploring the area allows visitors to witness the delicate balance of nature and the importance of preserving these natural environments for future generations.

Kawasan Falls is a true gem of the Philippines, offering visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, adventure, and serenity. Whether you seek a thrilling canyoneering experience, a peaceful escape into nature, or simply a chance to witness the wonders of the environment, Kawasan Falls promises an unforgettable journey. Embrace the splendor of this natural wonder and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Location

To reach Kawasan Falls, there are a number of airlines that offer direct flights Manila to Cebu City, including Philippine Airlines, Cebu Pacific, and AirAsia. Flight time is approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes.


From Cebu City, take a bus at Cebu South Bus Terminal or at Librando Trans Inc. It will take about 3 hours of travel to reach Badian. You need to hike 15 to 30 minutes from the entry point in Barangay Matutinao to Kawasan Falls.
