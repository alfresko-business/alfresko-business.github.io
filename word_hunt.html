Word Hunt

A fun and exciting game that will test your knowledge of tourist destinations in the beautiful Philippines! This game features a grid of letters with 13 hidden words for you to discover.

Overview

This game is a great way to challenge yourself and see just how familiar you are with some of the most popular tourist destinations in the Philippines. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or simply love exploring new places, this game is sure to keep you engaged and entertained.


Instructions

  1. To play, simply search the grid for the words listed below. You'll need to be quick and observant to find them all! As you find each word, mark it off the list and keep searching until you find all 13.
  2. Remember, these words can be found in any direction - horizontally, vertically, diagonally forwards, or backwards. So, keep your eyes peeled and your mind sharp! Have fun and good luck on your word hunt.
