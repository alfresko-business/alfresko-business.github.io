Tourist Spot Destinations in Visayas

Chocolate Hills


The Chocolate Hills are a geological marvel located in the Bohol province of the Philippines. This natural wonder is composed of over 1,200 perfectly symmetrical hills, which turn a chocolate brown color during the dry season. It is considered one of the country's most iconic and visited tourist destinations.

Boracay White Beach


Boracay's White Beach is a stunning 4-kilometer stretch of white, powdery sand that slopes gently into crystal-clear waters. Located on the western side of the island, it's the perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and watching the breathtaking sunset. The beach is lined with resorts, restaurants, and bars, offering a lively atmosphere day and night.
