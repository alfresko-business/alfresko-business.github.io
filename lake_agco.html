Lake Agco

Place: Kidapawan City, North Cotabato

What Makes It Unique: Lake Agco is located at the foot of Mount Apo, the highest peak in the Philippines. This proximity offers visitors the opportunity to combine their trip to the hot springs with a hike up Mount Apo, providing a diverse and exciting adventure for outdoor enthusiasts.

Overview

Lake Agco is a natural lake located at the foot of Mount Apo in Ilomavis, Kidapawan City in Mindanao, Philippines. It is a blend of calm lake and thick forests which make this a lovable place to visit and stay. The area where the lake is located is the Sacred Tribal and Ancestral Grounds of Manobo Tribes of Mount Apo.

One of the highlights of Lake Agco is its geothermal features. The area is blessed with natural hot springs and steaming pools, providing a therapeutic and relaxing experience for visitors. The warm and mineral-rich waters are believed to have healing properties and are perfect for soothing tired muscles and rejuvenating the body and mind.

Visitors to Lake Agco can choose from several accommodations, ranging from simple cottages to camping grounds. The serene ambiance and the cool mountain climate make it an ideal destination for a relaxing getaway, whether it's a day trip or an extended stay. In addition to its natural beauty, Lake Agco is also home to a number of cultural attractions. The Manobo tribes who live in the area have a rich culture and history. Visitors can learn about the tribe's culture and traditions through a number of cultural shows and exhibits.

As Lake Agco is situated within a protected area, it is important for visitors to respect the environment and follow responsible tourism practices. Leave no trace, take only memories, and preserve the natural wonders that make Lake Agco a true gem in the heart of Mindanao.

Location

To reach Lake Agco, take an airplane bound to Davao International Airport. From there, you can take a bus from Ecoland Bus Terminal bound for Kidapawan City. Several bus companies operate regular trips between the two cities. The travel time is approximately 2-3 hours, depending on traffic conditions.


From Kidapawan City, you can take a walk, jeepney or tricycle to the Ilomavis area. The walk is about 1 kilometer long and takes about 15 minutes. The motorcycle ride is about 5 minutes long.
