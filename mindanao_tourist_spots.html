Tourist Spot Destinations in Mindanao

Aliwagwag Falls


Aliwagwag Falls, located in the southern Philippines, is a stunning natural wonder with a series of cascading waterfalls and pools that will take your breath away. The falls are known for their unique stair-step formation, creating a mesmerizing display of rushing water against a backdrop of verdant foliage. It's a must-see destination for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.

Lake Sebu


Lake Sebu, nestled in the lush mountains of South Cotabato, Philippines, is a breathtaking gem of tranquility. Surrounded by mist-covered hills, this enchanting lake offers a serene retreat. Boasting crystal-clear waters, it is home to vibrant floating villages and rich biodiversity. Visitors can explore its beauty through boat rides, encounter indigenous T'boli culture, and savor delicious tilapia dishes.

The Bukidnon-Davao Road, a scenic route connecting the provinces of Bukidnon and Davao in the Philippines, traverses breathtaking landscapes and offers an adventurous journey. It winds through verdant mountains, rolling hills, and picturesque valleys, providing stunning panoramic views along the way. Travelers can immerse themselves in the region's natural beauty and discover charming towns and vibrant local communities.

T'boli Village


T'boli Village, nestled in the picturesque highlands of southern Philippines, is a captivating cultural enclave. Home to the indigenous T'boli people, the village showcases their vibrant traditions, intricate craftsmanship, and colorful attire. Visitors can immerse themselves in T'boli music, dance, and weaving, while exploring the rich heritage and warm hospitality of this indigenous community.
